About Minesweeper Playground

Minesweeper Playground is a web-based version of the classic Minesweeper game, designed to help beginners practice their programming skills.

The game features a randomly generated minefield, where players must carefully uncover squares without detonating any mines. As they progress, players can compete on the leaderboard and track their scores.
