About the Game

Minesweeper is a classic logic-based game where the objective is to clear a rectangular board containing hidden mines without detonating any of them. Players reveal squares on the board, and if a square with a mine is revealed, the game ends. If no mines are revealed, a number is displayed indicating how many neighboring squares contain mines.

In this web-based version, you can practice your Minesweeper skills and compete with others on the leaderboard.
